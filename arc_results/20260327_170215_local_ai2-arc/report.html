About This Benchmark

ARC-AGI (Abstraction & Reasoning Corpus): Grid-based pattern recognition tasks designed to resist memorization. Each task requires discovering a novel rule from 3-5 training examples and applying it to a test input. Scored by exact grid match only.

AI2-ARC (Allen Institute): Science multiple-choice questions split into Easy and Challenge sets. Challenge questions specifically require multi-step reasoning and cannot be answered by simple retrieval.

Local mode: Oricli-Alpha full stack (MCTS, CoT, safety, memory) with Ollama small models.
RunPod mode: Large vLLM model (AWQ quantized), direct inference, no Oricli reasoning stack.
The gap between the two isolates the value of the reasoning architecture vs. raw model scale.
