## Summary
Implement the hybrid reasoning system for the Abstraction and Reasoning Corpus (ARC), combining **Induction** (Program Synthesis) and **Transduction** (Neural Prediction). This system is based on the paper "Combining Induction and Transduction for Abstract Reasoning" (arxiv:2411.02272) and aims to achieve state-of-the-art results on the ARC benchmark.
